I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Northeastern University, specializing in low-power Analog, RF, Mixed-Signal, and Photonics IC design. My engineering focus spans the entire IC development cycle—from advanced architecture and circuit design to layout implementation and rigorous silicon validation. 

## Core Expertise & Technical Skills
* **IC Design & EDA Tools:** Experienced with Cadence Virtuoso, Spectre, ORCAD PSpice, ADS, HFSS, MATLAB, Simulink, and TCAD.
* **Subsystem Architecture:** Proficient in BAW/SAW Filter design, LNAs, PAs, PLLs, Mixers, Bandgap References (BGR), OTAs, LDOs, Rectifiers, Matching Networks, and Oscillators.
* **Verification & Lab Skills:** Skilled in schematic and layout implementation, SI/PI analysis, EM simulation, and RF hardware characterization using VNAs, Oscilloscopes, and Spectrum Analyzers.
